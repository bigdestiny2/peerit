## Board Brief

- Title: Cypherpunk
- Audience: Bitcoin, Lightning, privacy engineering, and censorship-resistance builders.
- Launch role: Narrative engine for signed records and PoW-gated posting.
- Founder brief: Keep discussion technical and lawful. No darknet-market positioning.
- Moderation risk: medium

## Deliverables

- Create one owner/moderator identity for r/cypherpunk.
- Publish 12 quality starter posts before paid traffic.
- Publish at least 4 open-ended discussion prompts.
- Pin one norms post and exercise the moderation overlay before launch.
- Reply daily during the first launch week so the board does not feel empty.
