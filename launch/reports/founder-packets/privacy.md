## Board Brief

- Title: Privacy
- Audience: PrivacyGuides, degoogle, Tor, Brave, and no-account software users.
- Launch role: Core message fit for no-account posting and local identity.
- Founder brief: Keep claims precise: no central account, signed identities, public boards, and no magic anonymity promises.
- Moderation risk: medium

## Deliverables

- Create one owner/moderator identity for r/privacy.
- Publish 15 quality starter posts before paid traffic.
- Publish at least 4 open-ended discussion prompts.
- Pin one norms post and exercise the moderation overlay before launch.
- Reply daily during the first launch week so the board does not feel empty.

## Pinned Norms Post Template

```markdown
# Welcome to r/privacy

This board is for PrivacyGuides, degoogle, Tor, Brave, and no-account software users.

Bring concrete links, demos, build notes, questions, and careful comparisons.
Keep claims precise: Peerit has no central account, uses signed identities, and gates posting with small proof-of-work.
Do not position this board around illegal content, harassment, doxxing, ban evasion, or "anything goes" moderation.

Read on the gateway. Post through PearBrowser.
```
